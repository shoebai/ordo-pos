Local Network Setup
Run Ordo POS on your PC — all devices on the same WiFi sync instantly. No internet required.
1
Download the package — a ZIP containing the server and app files.
2
Extract the ZIP to any folder on your Windows PC (e.g. Desktop).
3
Double-click setup.bat — it installs Node.js automatically and creates a Desktop shortcut.
4
Click "Ordo POS" on your Desktop — opens at localhost:3000. Other devices connect via your PC's IP address.
